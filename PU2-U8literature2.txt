The bus went higher and higher. Tom saw his town below.|公共汽车开得越来越高。汤姆看到了下面的城镇。
It got smaller and smaller.|它变得越来越小。
‘Oh look! There’s the sports centre! And there’s the market!’ he said.|“哦看！那是体育中心！还有那是市场！”他说。
The bus went up and down like a ride at the funfair.|公共汽车上下起伏，就像游乐场里的游乐设施。
After a few minutes, Brenda said, ‘OK, get ready.|几分钟后，布伦达说：“好的，准备好。
We’re coming down to the bus stop.’|我们要下到公交站台了。”
At that moment, Tom heard his mum say, ‘Tom! Tom! You have to get up.|在那时，汤姆听到他的妈妈说：“汤姆！汤姆！你必须起床了。
We don’t want to be late. You have to catch the bus.’|我们不想迟到。你必须去赶公共汽车。”
‘Oh no!’, Tom thought. ‘It was all a dream.’|“哦不！”汤姆想。“那全是一场梦。”
‘Do I have to catch the bus, Mum?’ Tom asked.|“我必须去赶公共汽车吗，妈妈？”汤姆问。
‘Yes, you do. But don’t worry!’ his mum answered.|“是的，你必须。但是别担心！”他妈妈回答。
Finally, the school bus came. It was small and red.|最后，校车来了。它很小，是红色的。
The doors opened.|门打开了。
Tom looked up and saw Brenda from his dream.|汤姆抬头看，看到了梦里的布伦达。
‘Good morning, Tom. Welcome. I’m Brenda and this is your bus buddy, Bruno!’|“早上好，汤姆。欢迎。我是布伦达，这是你的校车伙伴，布鲁诺！”
‘Brenda! Bruno!’ Tom said and he got on the bus.|“布伦达！布鲁诺！”汤姆说，然后他上了公共汽车。
